The Caddy

Three things. Done quietly.

I.

Archive

Tabs you haven't touched in 24 hours are moved to the Vault automatically. Your browser stays clean. Nothing is lost, just waiting.

II.

Search

Open Caddy, type a word. Every tab archived in the last session, last week, last month, surfaces instantly. Type econ and find three tabs from your midterm prep. Type climate and your essay research reappears.

III.

Restore

Your calendar says English Essay at 5 pm. Caddy reads it, finds the matching tabs in your vault, and has a notification waiting the moment you sit down. Press Restore. You're back in it.

✦

Why Caddy exists

"You spend an hour finding the perfect sources. Then life happens. By the time you open your laptop for the study block, every tab is gone and you're Googling the same things again."

Most productivity tools remind you to work. None of them put you back in the exact place you left. Existing tab managers let you store tabs, but none of them help you actually reuse them. Caddy is built for that second step: not a workspace organizer, but a memory layer for your browser that knows what you need and when you need it.
